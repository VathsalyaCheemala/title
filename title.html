Story of Jesus

Jesus was born in Bethlehem to Mary, conceived miraculously by the Holy Spirit, and grew up in Nazareth.
At around 30, He began His ministry, teaching about love, forgiveness, and the Kingdom of God, while performing miracles like healing the sick and raising the dead.

His growing influence led to conflict with religious leaders, and He was betrayed, arrested, and crucified under Roman authority.

Three days after His death, Jesus rose from the dead, appearing to His followers and proving His victory over sin and death.
He later ascended to heaven, promising to return, and His life and teachings continue to inspire billions as the foundation of Christianity.
